McDonald's Menu Canada 2026: Full Pricing & New Items Guide

McDonald's Canada makes it really hard to find prices. Their app hides them, and the website isn't much better. So here's a simple guide to what things cost in Canadian dollars and what's worth buying — whether you're feeding your family, grabbing lunch, or trying to save money.

One thing to know: prices are not the same at every location. A meal in Vancouver can cost more than the same meal in a small town in Manitoba. This happens because of local wages, who runs the restaurant, and where it's located. So use these prices as a good guide, but not an exact number.

McDonald's Breakfast Menu Canada 2026

Breakfast at McDonald's Canada is one of the best deals of the day, especially if you know what to skip and what to grab. Breakfast is usually available from 5:00 AM to 10:49 AM on weekdays and until 11:00 AM on weekends, with most items priced between CA$2.39 and CA$7.99.

One of the most common mistakes I see is people thinking they can get breakfast items at any time. McDonald's Canada got rid of all-day breakfast a while back. Most places stop serving breakfast around 10:30 AM.

If you are planning a late morning visit, go before that time or you will end up with the lunch menu instead. Popular breakfast items include the Egg McMuffin, Sausage McMuffin with Egg, Hotcakes, Hash Browns, and the Breakfast Burrito. The Egg McMuffin Combo costs around CA$7.69, which is a good deal for a full breakfast.

Regional Price Differences Across Canadian Provinces

If you have ever ordered the same meal in two different provinces and noticed the price was not the same, that is not an error. Prices at McDonald's can be different depending on the province, city, type of place, and even the person who owns that McDonald's.

The biggest reason for this is minimum wage. Provinces where workers get paid more, like Ontario and British Columbia, tend to have slightly higher food prices to cover those extra costs. The difference on one item might be CA$0.20 to CA$0.50, but on a big family order it starts to add up.

Airport locations are a completely different thing. Expect to pay a lot more at any McDonald's inside an airport in Canada, no matter which province you are in. That higher price is normal at all food places in airports and has nothing to do with the local wage rate.

McDonald's Canada Halal, Vegetarian & Allergen Options

There is a lot of confusion about this topic online, so here is the simplest answer I can give you.

On halal: McDonald's Canada does not serve certified halal food at all locations. A small number of places in cities like Toronto and Mississauga have served halal food in the past, but this is not the same at every location. Call your nearby McDonald's directly before thinking they have halal food. Do not trust other apps or old websites to tell you if a specific location has halal food.

On vegetarian options, items that are usually found at most Canadian locations include:

  • Fruit and Maple Oatmeal
  • Side Salad
  • Hash Browns
  • Apple Slices
  • World Famous Fries, which are seen as vegetarian friendly in Canada
  • McVeggie or Spicy McVeggie sandwich, depending on the location

On allergens: McDonald's Canada does not have a special menu for people who cannot eat gluten. Most main food items have gluten in them because of the bun or coating. If you have a bad food allergy, check the Health Canada allergy rules and talk to the manager at that location before you order.

FAQs - McDonald's Menur

  1. How much does a Big Mac cost in Canada in 2026?
    A Big Mac costs approximately CA$8.97 as a solo item. As a combo meal it runs around CA$11.29 to CA$12.49 depending on your location and province.
  2. What time does McDonald's breakfast end in Canada?
    Most locations end breakfast between 10:30 AM and 11:00 AM. Weekends typically run slightly later. Hours vary by franchise so check your local location before heading out.
  3. Is McDonald's more expensive in Canada than in the US?
    Yes. McDonald's Canada is roughly 4% more expensive than the US when prices are converted to USD. A Big Mac that costs around US$5.99 in the US runs CA$8.97 in Canada.
  4. Does McDonald's Canada have all-day breakfast?
    No. All-day breakfast is no longer standard at Canadian locations. Breakfast is served during morning hours only at most restaurants.
  5. What is the cheapest item on the McDonald's Canada menu?
    A Hot Tea at CA$1.93 is currently the most affordable item. The Vanilla Cone and Baked Apple Pie are also consistently among the lowest-priced options on the menu.
  6. Can I use McDonald's app deals with Uber Eats or SkipTheDishes?
    No. App-based deals from McDonald's Canada are not valid through third-party delivery platforms. You need to order through the McDonald's app directly, in-store, or at the drive-thru.
  7. Does McDonald's Canada use Canadian beef?
    Yes. McDonald's Canada uses 100% Canadian beef, Grade A eggs, and locally grown potatoes for its fries and hash browns.
  8. What delivery options does McDonald's Canada offer?
    Delivery is available through McDelivery, Uber Eats, SkipTheDishes, and DoorDash across most Canadian cities. Expect a 10 to 25% markup on menu prices when ordering through third-party platforms, plus service and delivery fees on top.
